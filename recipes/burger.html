Burger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe to follow if you intend to make the best burgers in the entire world. It involves very simple steps you have to follow in order to call yourself the Burger King

Buckle up. Recipe's about to start

Ingredients
  • some meat
  • burger buns
  • salad
  • tomato
  • burger sauce

Steps

  1. form the meat into small patties and put them into a pan to cook
  2. cut the tomato into round pieces
  3. wash the salad and cut it into small pieces suitable to put on top of the burger
  4. put burger sauce on both halfs of the burger bun
  5. assamble the burger
